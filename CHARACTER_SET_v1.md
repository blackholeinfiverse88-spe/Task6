Role: Support / Buffer

Concept: A stout, powerful nature spirit guarding hidden temple treasures. Instead of runes, he uses Sacred Mantras to fortify the earth around allies.

2. Gandharva Songstress (Fairy Archetype)
Role: Pure Healer / Crowd Control

Concept: A celestial musician whose Veena music can knit wounds or entrance enemies.

Visual: Floating gracefully, draped in flowing silk (Sari style), surrounded by a divine aura and peacock feathers.

3. Naga Infiltrator (Goblin Archetype)
Role: Utility / Chaos / Poison

Concept: A half-human, half-serpentine dweller of Patala (the underworld). Uses alchemy and venomous traps rather than mechanical gadgets.

Visual: Hooded cobra features, multiple arms holding vials of corrosive venom and jagged daggers.

Visual: Sharp fangs, ornate but dark regal clothing, casting purple illusory flames.
